Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your PackSnap account and all associated data at any time, directly from within the app or by contacting support.

⚠️ Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your boxes, items, and account data will be erased immediately.

1 Delete from within the app

  1. Open the PackSnap app on your device
  2. Tap the Profile tab (bottom navigation)
  3. Scroll down to the Account section
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Type DELETE in the confirmation field
  6. Tap Permanently Delete Account

2 Request deletion by email

If you are unable to access the app, email us at support@getpacksnap.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 7 days.

What data is deleted

  • Your account profile (name, email)
  • All boxes and items you have created
  • All photos associated with your items
  • Your subscription and billing history (managed by Apple / Google)

Data retention

Upon deletion, all personal data is removed immediately from our systems. Subscription billing records are retained by Apple App Store or Google Play in accordance with their own policies. PackSnap retains no data after deletion.
